Refund Policy

Pre-booking:

SkillCentral offers a 100% refund for our upskilling courses if the refund request is made within the first 3 days of registration, referred to as the Demo Duration. After this 3-day period, no refunds will be provided under any circumstances.

To request a "4th day no question asked" refund, please follow the procedure below:

• Send an email to: enquiry@websoc.ai with the subject line “course refund.”

• Send the refund request email from your registered email ID only.

Paid via a loan:

SkillCentral provides a 100% refund or loan cancellation if the request is made within 3 days of registration. After this 3-day period, no refunds will be provided.

To request a "4th day no question asked" refund, please follow the procedure below:

• Send an email to: enquiry@websoc.ai with the subject line “course refund.”

• Send the refund request email from your registered email ID only.

One-shot/Partial payment:

SkillCentral provides a 100% refund for our upskilling courses if the request is raised within 3 days of registration during the Full Course Duration. After this 3-day period, no refunds will be issued.

To request a "4th day no question asked" refund, please follow the procedure below:

• Send an email to: enquiry@websoc.ai with the subject line “course refund.”

• Send the refund request email from your registered email ID only.

Paid via a loan:

SkillCentral provides a 100% refund or loan cancellation if the request is made within 3 days of registration. After this 3-day period, no refunds will be provided.

To request a "4th day no question asked" refund, please follow the procedure below:

• Send an email to: enquiry@websoc.ai with the subject line “course refund.”

• Send the refund request email from your registered email ID only.

Other Points to Consider for Refund:

• If students enrolled in the offline mode fail to send a cancellation email within the 3-day window period, a seat cost of 10,000 + GST will be charged monthly.

• Changing the course is possible during the demo course access. However, it is not possible after making full payment, after the loan is disbursed, or after full course access is granted, and no refund has been initiated.

• The loan tenure, monthly EMI, and KYC completion are solely the customer's responsibility. Once the loan is approved, any changes in the EMI plan or loan tenure are managed by the loan partner and the customer. SkillCentral does not participate in these modifications.

• Changing the mode of learning from online to offline is possible during the demo course access. Once full course access is obtained, switching is not allowed. In emergency cases, a fee of 30,000 will be charged for changing the course.

• Only email communication to enquiry@websoc.ai is accepted for refund requests. Refund requests made via chat, message, or phone call will not be accepted or considered.
